Frequently Asked Questions

What types of surgical instruments do you supply?

We supply a comprehensive range of surgical instruments including scissors, forceps, clamps, retractors, surgical blades, needles, and specialized instruments for various medical procedures.

Do you provide custom-made instruments?

Yes, we offer custom manufacturing services for specialized surgical instruments. Our team can work with you to design and produce instruments that meet your specific requirements.

What certifications do your products have?

All our products are ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 13485:2016 certified, with CE marking and FDA registration. We maintain the highest quality standards in all our manufacturing processes.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide worldwide shipping to healthcare institutions globally. We have partnerships with reliable logistics providers to ensure timely and secure delivery.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We work with both small and large orders. For standard products, we can accommodate orders as small as individual pieces. For custom manufacturing, minimum quantities may apply.
